## Description

Stop reading about public speaking. Start doing it — out loud, on the record, and measured.

Podium is a four-week college public speaking course that's taught, drilled, and graded entirely by AI — with most assignments performed at the mic instead of typed. Read the lecture, step up to a prompt, record your spoken answer, and get coached on both *what* you said and *how* you said it.

Every recording is transcribed automatically and scored on two dimensions — content and delivery — with real metrics pulled straight from your voice: words per minute, filler-word rate, pauses, pace variation, and fluency. No vibes. Just a coach's note you can act on before your next take.

▶ What you get:
• A full month of public speaking — 29 topics across four units, from managing nerves to a capstone speech
• Spoken-first homework and tests — record audio or video right in the browser
• A two-dimensional grade — content and delivery scored separately, then blended into an overall mark
• Delivery metrics from the audio — WPM, filler words, pauses, fluency, and pace variation
• Written reflections where they fit — audience analysis and rehearsal planning, graded on content
• Live progress analytics — per-unit mastery, best scores, and a recent-activity feed
• Fail-loud grading — if the AI can't grade it honestly, it tells you instead of faking a score

Podium is where the lecture, the prompt, the microphone, and the coach all live in one room.

Get measurably better at speaking — week over week, take after take.
